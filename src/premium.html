Perk Descriptions

Below is a list of all premium perks and what they mean.

(Click on a perk to display more information about it)

Your previous prefix will be overridden with the <Premium> prefix
Allows you to change your name in game to a custom one of your choosing using /nick
Allows you to carry a virtual crafting table around on you at all times, which can be accessed using /craft
Allows you to carry a virtual link to your enderchest that can be accessed anywhere using /echest
Allows you to use most items and blocks in the game using /hat while holding the item or block in your hand
Allows you to immediately condense all resources in your inventory (if possible) into blocks using /condense
Allows you to spawn in an infinite number of player skulls of your own player using /skull
Allows you to choose from a range of different particle effects within the cosmetic menu
Allows you to choose from a range of different arrow particle effects from the cosmetic menu that trigger when an arrow is fired
Allows you to choose from a range of different balloons variants from within the cosmetic menu that float around you
Allows you to choose from a range of different emote faces from the cosmetic menu which take the form of an animated player head you can wear
Allows you to change the colour of the chat you type
Allows you to choose from a range of different death effects within the cosmetic menu which trigger when you die
Allows you to summon a noteblock DJ who will play a song of your choosing from within the cosmetic menu
Allows you to choose from a range of different gadgets within the cosmetic menu. These include trampolines, rockets, and paint ball guns
Allows you to choose from a range of summonable pets within the cosmetic menu. These pets will follow you around where ever you go
Allows you to choose from a range of different miniatures within the cosmetic menu. These minatures represent small mobs that will hover around you. And similar to pets, Miniatures will also follow you around where ever you go
Allows you to choose from a range of pre-set banners within the cosmetic menu to wear on your head similar to a hat
Allows you to add decoration to your chat such as bold, italic, and underlined formatting
Prevents the server kicking you when going AFK or idle for more than 5 minutes
Allows you to set 6 homes (places to teleport to) on the server as opposed to the default amount of 3
Allows you to talk sarcastically in chat using /sarcasm

* Depending on the server, some premium benifits will not be accessible
