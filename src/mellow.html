Mellow

A new way to define the survival experience in Minecraft. Mellow gives Minecraft survival a much needed enhancing by adding new mechanics and elements to this gamemode. In doing so, it makes it much more enjoyable to play and it gives the player more than one goal to strive for. In addition, some vanilla mechanics have been tweaked.

Current Features

Below are a list of features mellow currently has. This list is not final and is expected to change from update to update.

(Click on a feature to display more information about it)

Freshy generated 1.14.4 terrain. We decided to use the default terrain instead of custom terrain because of the features that default terrain now generates with. This includes the aquatic life and plants, underwater structures like ocean monuments and shipwrecks, the newly styled villages, and many other randomly generated structures that would not otherwise be available had we used custom terrain.
Using regular eggs, you have a chance to capture a mob by throwing an egg at them. This chance is very high for passive mobs like sheep, cows, and pigs. But very low for hostile mobs such as zombies, skeletons, and spiders. No you cannont capture any boss mobs.
Gone are the days were you had to click a button in order to respawn yourself. Upon death, black smoke will appear and you will respawn automatically.
A small feature which has the chance to give you extra ore when mining an ore block. This only affects ore blocks that were generated by the world and not ores that have been placed by a player.
Think that minecarts are pretty slow? We do too! Placing a redstone block under a powered rail will vastly increase the speed of a minecart. This is made more effective with multiple redstone blocks and powered rails in a straight line. Do beware that a minecart going at high speeds around bends will not end well.
An interface that allows you to exchange goods with players who are near you safely wihout the need to drop items on the ground.
A system that allows you to level up your equipment and attatch modifiers to them to make them perform better. Equipment includes Swords, Axes, Pickaxes, Shovels, Bows, Hoes, Shears, Fishingrods, and Armour. A list of available modifiers can be seen using /mt mods. You may also view this webpage which explain what each modifier does. https://minetinker.flo56958.de/modifiers.html
A mechanic that allows you to instantly move up and down floors without the need for pesky stairs. This can be accomplished by placing emerald blocks vertically, leaving gaps inbetween.
A new way to transport between all the dimensions of Minecraft! Flying higher than 1000 in the overworld will transport you to the end. Falling lower than -50 in the overworld will transport you to the nether. This also works in a simelar way while in the end and nether dimensions.
When dropping an item from your inventory, the item will display a hologram of its name (custom display name if it has one) above itself.
Various tweaks have been made to the default vanilla game play.
  • Every mob in the game (including the sub-varients of a mob like parrots and rabbits) have the chance to drop their head when killed by a player. The chance varies differently from mob to mob.
  • If a villager dies somewhere, it will be announced in chat.
  • When the ender dragon is killed, it will drop its head, a dragon egg, and an elytra.
  • Shulkers drop 2 shulker shells on death instead of 1.
When talking in chat, the message you recently sent will also pop up above your head and can be viewable by others who are near you.

Upcoming Features

Below are a list of upcoming features for mellow that are still in development but are due to be released at some point in a future update. This list is not final and is expected to change from update to update.

(Click on a feature to display more information about it)

A system that allows you to sell items on a virtual market for as much as you want. Players have the option to sell/buy through the exchange or through the server. The exchange can be used to sell any item custom or not for as much or as little as the player wants. When another player buys their item from the exchange, the seller will receive coin they requested for the item and the buyer will receive their goods. Items being sold through the server can be sold immediately for coin. However, the server keeps track of the finite amount of resources it has bought from players. You cannot buy an infinite amount of resources from the server. You can only buy from the server what has been sold to it buy other players.
Structures will appear across mellow depicting of ruins or old shacks. Players can enter these to encounter a custom boss mob to fight. If a player is successful in defeating the boss mob, they are able to charge other players a fixed fee for entering the boss room until another player successfully defeats the boss mob again.
Allows you to lend an item to another player for a set amount of time or until they log out. The lender can also set a maximum amount of durability that can be used if the item being lent has durability.
Allows the use of special crafting that respects item meta data, allowing the player to craft special items
Allows you to train many skills which provide benifits and perk points. An example being the attack skill, which increases your base damage by a set amount each level.
